Hi, I'm Kim

I'm a Front End Developer & Interactive Designer

I've been designing and developing for the web for a little over 5 years now. I got my start in design by taking a few conceptual design classes right out of high school and presented most of my homework or "comps" as acrylic paintings. I had no idea how to use a design program, push pixels or read code. But, I did have ideas. Now I work as a senior designer for an ecommerce software company called Volusion. There, I dabble in UX and UI for small and large merchants selling an array of products.

I'm also diving into front-end development so I can master both skillsets. Learning from a one-on-one mentorship program called Thinkful, I've been able to build up a steady collection of development snippets.


When I'm not designing or diving into new skillsets I'm usually out and about in the city of Austin, TX. There's quite a bit to do here, and my search for sunshine and great patio drink never go unfound.
